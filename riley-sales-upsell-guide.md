Christopher meets with you to understand:

**Timeline:** Depends on the complexity of your service menu and how many upsell flows you want configured. Simple (1-2 upsell scenarios) can be live quickly. More complex (multiple services, multiple objection paths) takes longer. Christopher discusses timeline during the discovery call.

## Common Questions

**"Can the AI handle objections?"**
Yes. We configure specific objection responses based on the most common pushbacks your clients give. The AI doesn't just make the offer and go silent — it knows how to respond if they hesitate.

**"What if my services change?"**
We update the script. Your business evolves and your AI should too. Script updates are handled by the Topher AI team.

**"Can I hear it before it goes live?"**
Yes. We run test calls and you approve the script before it goes live on your line.

**"Will it sound like a robot trying to sell me something?"**
No — and this is where the configuration matters. The script is written in your brand voice, triggered at natural moments in the conversation, and designed to feel like a helpful suggestion rather than a pitch. The goal is for callers to feel served, not sold to.

**"What if the caller just wants to book and leave?"**
The AI reads the room. If a caller is clearly in a rush or has given strong signals they just want to complete their booking, the upsell moment gets skipped. We configure thresholds for this.

**"How do I know if it's working?"**
Every call is logged in your lead dashboard. You can see which calls triggered the upsell offer, how callers responded, and track conversions over time.
