What AI Handles for Insurance Agencies

1

24/7 Lead Capture

AI answers inbound calls, web form leads, and chat inquiries instantly — capturing name, contact info, coverage type of interest, and ZIP code before routing to an agent.

2

Coverage Type Triage

AI identifies what type of coverage the prospect needs — auto, home, renters, life, commercial, health — and routes to the appropriate specialist agent.

3

Pre-Quote Info Gathering

AI collects the information agents need to prepare quotes: current insurer, coverage history, property/vehicle details, number of drivers, and coverage preferences.

4

Consultation Scheduling

AI books a quote review call or in-person meeting directly into the agent's calendar — eliminating phone tag.

5

Renewal Reminder Campaigns

AI sends automated renewal reminders 60, 30, and 7 days before policy expiration — reducing lapses and preventing clients from shopping competitors.
