Reducing No-Shows with AI

The exact reminder sequences, channels, and timing that cut no-show rates by 30–50%.

Quick Answer

Automated AI reminder sequences reduce no-shows by 30–50% by sending timely, personalized messages with easy rescheduling options.

The formula: instant booking confirmation + 24-hour reminder + 2-hour day-of reminder — all via SMS, all automated, all with a one-tap reschedule link.

The Optimal Reminder Sequence

1

Instant Booking Confirmation

Sent immediately when appointment is booked. Sets expectations and captures commitment.

"Hi [Name]! Your [Treatment] appointment at [Spa] is confirmed for [Date] at [Time]. Reply C to confirm or R to reschedule. See you then! 💆"
2

24-Hour Reminder

Sent the day before. Highest impact reminder — this is where most no-shows are prevented.

"Reminder: You have a [Treatment] appointment tomorrow at [Time] with [Provider]. Need to reschedule? Reply R or call [Number]. We look forward to seeing you!"
3

Day-Of Reminder (2 Hours Before)

Catches last-minute cancellations early enough to fill the slot from a waitlist.

"See you in 2 hours, [Name]! Your [Treatment] appointment is at [Time]. Parking is [Location]. Can't make it? Reply CANCEL so we can offer your spot to another client."
4

No-Show Recovery (If Missed)

Sent 30 minutes after a missed appointment. Offers easy rebooking without judgment.

"Hi [Name], we missed you today! Life happens — no worries. Would you like to reschedule your [Treatment]? Reply YES and we'll find a new time for you."
