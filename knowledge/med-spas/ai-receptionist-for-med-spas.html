Quick Answer

AI handles the full med spa client journey — from first inquiry to post-treatment follow-up — without adding front desk hours.

What AI Does for Med Spas

1

24/7 Inquiry Response

AI answers calls, texts, and web chat inquiries instantly — including evenings and weekends when most prospects search for med spa treatments.

2

Treatment FAQ Handling

AI answers questions about Botox, fillers, laser treatments, CoolSculpting, chemical peels, and more — pricing, what to expect, recovery, and candidacy.

3

Appointment Booking

AI books directly into your scheduling system (Jane, Mindbody, Aesthetix, or similar) — no back-and-forth email chains.

4

Pre-Care Instructions

AI automatically sends treatment-specific pre-care reminders via SMS — reducing day-of cancellations caused by missed prep instructions.

5

Post-Visit Follow-Up & Rebooking

AI checks in 48–72 hours post-treatment, asks about their experience, and offers to rebook follow-up treatments or maintenance appointments.
