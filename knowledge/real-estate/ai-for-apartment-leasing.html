AI Receptionist for Apartment Leasing

Automate prospect qualification, tour scheduling, and follow-up — without adding headcount.

Quick Answer

AI handles the full leasing inquiry funnel — from first contact to booked tour — without a human leasing agent.

When a prospect inquires about a unit, AI engages immediately, qualifies them (move-in date, unit size, budget, pets), answers common questions, and schedules a tour — all in the same conversation.

What AI Handles in Leasing

1

Instant Lead Response

AI responds to web forms, ILS leads (Zillow, Apartments.com), and phone calls within seconds — day or night.

2

Prospect Qualification

AI collects move-in date, occupant count, unit size preference, income range, and pet ownership — only Fair Housing–compliant questions.

3

Unit Matching & FAQs

AI answers pricing, availability, amenity, pet policy, and lease term questions without a leasing agent getting on the phone.

4

Tour Scheduling

AI books in-person or self-guided tours directly into your property management system or calendar.

5

Automated Reminders & Follow-Up

AI sends tour reminders, re-engages no-shows, and follows up with prospects who didn't convert.
