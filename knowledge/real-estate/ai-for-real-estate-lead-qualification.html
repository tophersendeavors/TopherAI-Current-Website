AI for Real Estate Lead Qualification

Separate serious buyers and sellers from tire-kickers instantly — without hiring an ISA.

Quick Answer

AI qualifies real estate leads by asking the right questions at the exact moment of inquiry.

When a lead comes in, AI engages immediately — asking about pre-approval, timeline, budget, and motivation. It scores each lead and routes hot prospects to agents instantly, while nurturing cold leads via automated follow-up.

What AI Qualifies in a Real Estate Lead

Buyer Signals

  • Pre-approval status
  • Target move-in timeline
  • Price range and neighborhood
  • Current agent relationship
  • Motivation (job relocation, growing family, etc.)

Seller Signals

  • Expected listing timeline
  • Property type and condition
  • Already working with an agent?
  • Reason for selling
  • Price expectations

Sample AI Qualification Conversation

AI: Hi! I'm the AI assistant for Sunrise Realty. I saw you requested info on 412 Oak Ave — are you looking to buy, or just exploring the market?
Lead: Looking to buy, probably in the next 3 months.
AI: Great timeline! Have you gotten pre-approved for a mortgage yet?
Lead: Yes, up to $650k.
AI: Perfect. Are you currently working with a buyer's agent, or are you open to meeting with one of our team members?
Lead: No agent yet, open to it.
HOT LEAD — Pre-approved, 90-day timeline, no agent — routing to agent now
