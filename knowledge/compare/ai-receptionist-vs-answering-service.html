AI Receptionist vs. Answering Service

Both handle your calls. Only one qualifies leads, books appointments, and costs a flat monthly rate.

Quick Answer

Answering services relay messages. AI receptionists resolve calls — qualifying leads, booking appointments, and syncing data automatically.

Answering services are better for true emergency routing and complex live transfers. AI is better for anything involving lead capture, qualification, scheduling, and 24/7 scale — at a fraction of the cost.

When to Use Each

Choose AI Receptionist When…

  • You need 24/7 lead qualification
  • Appointment booking is a priority
  • You want CRM auto-sync
  • Call volume is moderate to high
  • You want predictable monthly costs
  • Automated follow-up sequences matter

Choose Answering Service When…

  • Calls require sensitive human judgment
  • Complex emergency routing is needed
  • Call volume is very low (<50/mo)
  • Industry requires human verification
  • Callers are elderly or prefer humans
